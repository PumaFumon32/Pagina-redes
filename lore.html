Lore de Bloodborne

!!!!ADVERTENCIA¡¡¡¡

Bloodborne es un juego en que el lore es bastante "Criptido" por decirlo de alguna manera, el director del juego lo hizo con esa intencion. Queria transmitir la sensacion de estar perdido y desorientado en un mundo decadente, por eso el lore es obtuso y te da informacion a trozos mediante descripciones de objetos, dialogos de personajes y el escenario.

No hay ningun lore oficial, el creador solo nos obliga a respetar la parte del lore que se muestre explicitamnete, y voy a dejar aqui unas citas del propio miyazaki en una entrevista que le hicieron en VG247 en 2016

"I have no intention in forcing any of the storyline upon any of the players out there, and there will be no official statements made about the 'true' story of the game." - Miyazaki
"First of all, yes, there is a perfect storyline in my head. However, I have no intention at all of enforcing that storyline to the players out there."
"Only those storyline elements that actually make it into the game are something that I need to force players to accept as a base for building up their own interpretation of the world. There are things in my head that aren't in the games, after all - so after that, it's all up to the players. I have no intention in forcing any of the storyline upon any of the players out there, and there will be no official statements made about the 'true' story of the game."
